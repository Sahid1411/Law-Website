WHO WE ARE?

Established in 2008, our law firm stands as a premier full-service, multi-disciplinary firm in India, offering a comprehensive range of services including transactional, regulatory, advisory, dispute resolution, and tax assistance. Our main offices are situated in New Delhi and Mumbai, allowing us to serve a diverse range of clients, including local and international companies, banks, financial institutions, funds, promoter groups, and public sector entities. Driven by the goal to enhance client service and provide practical legal advice along with dedicated representation across all sectors, our firm has carved out a distinct identity.
